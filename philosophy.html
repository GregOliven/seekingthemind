Our Vision

The vision for this website is to become a source of inspiration for other individuals who are seeking the mind behind the invisible hand on their pilgrimage through the land of commerce.

Our Mission

We want to share Matthew’s intellectual gift of connecting his Christian spirituality to present life freely with all people who are thinking about these issues as well. We hope these ideas will touch your mind and will inspire you to take a critical view of how you live your Christian faith in your business, family, and community.
